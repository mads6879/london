Mentorship is a fantastic way to get support for your personal growth and career development. Mentorship relationships can be rewarding to both people, personally and professionally. It's an opportunity to develop communication skills, expand your viewpoints, and consider new ways of approaching situations. And both partners can advance their careers in the process.

You should apply to be a mentor if you:

  • 1. Want to extend your professional network
  • 2. Want to contribute to the community
  • 3. You are ready to share expertise
  • 4. You want to get a new perspective and learn from your mentees

You should apply to be a mentee if you:

  • 1. Want to start career in software engineering
  • 2. Want to find a better job
  • 3. Want to be promoted at work
  • 4. Want to apply for a leadership position
  • 5. Need support in advancing your career
